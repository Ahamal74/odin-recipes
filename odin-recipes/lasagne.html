LASAGNE RECIPIE

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans (6 ounces each) tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 9 cooked lasagne noodles, drained
  • 3 cups ricotta or cottage cheese
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions:

  1. Brown Ground Beef:
    • In a large skillet, cook ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until beef is browned. Drain excess fat.
  2. Add Tomato Ingredients:
    • Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, water, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Layer the Lasagne:
    • Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
    • In a 9x13-inch baking dish, layer 3 lasagne noodles, 1/3 of the meat sauce, 1/3 of the ricotta or cottage cheese, 1/3 of the mozzarella, and 1/3 of the Parmesan. Repeat layers two more times.
  4. Bake:
    • Cover with foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes or until hot and bubbly.
  5. Let It Rest:
    • Let it stand for 10 minutes before cutting into squares and serving.
