RISOTTO RECIPE

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups Arborio rice
  • 1/2 cup white wine
  • 5 cups vegetable or chicken broth (warm)
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley (optional)
  1. Cook Mushrooms:
    • Heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a big pan.
    • Add mushrooms and cook until they're golden. Set them aside.
  2. Saut√© Onions and Garlic:
    • In the same pan, add another tablespoon of olive oil.
    • Put in chopped onions and cook until they look clear. Add minced garlic and cook for one more minute.
  3. Start Rice:
    • Add Arborio rice to the pan. Stir and cook for about 2 minutes until it looks a bit golden.
  4. Use Wine:
    • Pour in the white wine. Stir until it's mostly soaked up by the rice.
  5. Add Broth:
    • Start adding the warm broth, one scoop at a time. Stir often.
    • Wait until the liquid is mostly soaked up before adding more. Do this until the rice is soft but a little firm in the middle (about 18-20 minutes).
  6. Mix in Mushrooms:
    • In the last few minutes, mix in the cooked mushrooms.
  7. Finish It:
    • Take the pan off the heat. Stir in butter and Parmesan until the risotto looks creamy.
    • Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve:
    • If you have it, sprinkle fresh parsley on top.
    • Serve it hot!
